About Us

Discover Our
Restaurant Story

Located in downtown Salt Lake City, the Chalet Cafe is devoted to providing a friendly environment to relax and unwind. With fresh brewed coffee and tea and delectable desserts, the Chalet Cafe provides a comfortable environment to write your book, work from home, and enjoy an authentic cottagecore experience.

The Chalet Cafe is family-owned by two sisters who couldn't find a cozy coffee shop and decided to make one of their own. We hope you love it as much as we do!
